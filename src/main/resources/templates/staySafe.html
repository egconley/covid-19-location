Protect others. Protect yourself.

How it spreads

Person-to-person

Close contact (6 feet)

Through inhalation of respiratory droplets from coughs, sneezes, speech

Through asymptomatic individuals

Everyone Should

Wash your hands often

With soap AND water

20 seconds +

Cover all surfaces of your hands

Especially after being in a public place

Or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing

If no soap, hand sanitizer with at LEAST 60% alcohol (until dry)

Avoid touching eyes/nose/mouth with unwashed hands

Avoid close contact

Put distance between yourself and others

At LEAST 6 feet

Cover your mouth and nose in public

With a cloth face cover

Do NOT use cloth face coverings on children below age 2

Or on those who have trouble breathing or are unconscious/incapacitated

Do NOT use a facemask meant for a healthcare worker

Cover AND social distance

Cover coughs and sneezes

With a tissue

Or the inside of your elbow

Throw away used tissues

Cover AND social distance

Immediately wash your hands with soap and water

Or hand sanitizer (until dry)

20 seconds + !

Clean and disinfect

Frequently touched surfaces

DAILY!

Tables

Doorknobs

Light switches

Countertops

Handles

Desks

Phones

Keyboards

Toilets

Faucets

Sinks

Dirty surfaces

Detergent or soap and water prior to disinfection
